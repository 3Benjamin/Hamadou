The Team

Team WADABA team is a group of young aspiring African scholars from different nationalities. They are all experts in the field of Water who came together under the Pan African University Institute of Water and Energy Sciencesincluding climate change (PAUWES)-Algeria and developed an innovative idea-Water Data Bank, geared towards the provision of solutions related to water pollution in the African continent and Likasi in the Democratic Republic of Congo in particular.
